## 😊 Reflection

This run taught me an important lesson: a difficult start doesn't always mean it's going to be a bad run.

The first three kilometers felt surprisingly heavy, and I honestly questioned whether I would be able to complete the session. Instead of giving up or speeding up unnecessarily, I stayed patient and trusted the process.

Eventually my legs loosened up, my breathing became comfortable, and the remaining kilometers felt smooth and enjoyable.

Another lesson from today is that I'm reaching distances where hydration becomes much more important. For future runs longer than 8 KM, I should carry a larger water bottle.

Overall, this was one of my most satisfying easy runs so far.
