## 🤖 Coach Analysis

Excellent recovery day.

One of the biggest mistakes beginner runners make is turning every day into a hard training day. By choosing an active recovery session instead, you allowed your cardiovascular system and muscles to recover while still maintaining movement.

Your average heart rate of **104 bpm** indicates that the walk stayed well within a true recovery intensity.

Introducing basic leg strength exercises is another positive step. Building stronger glutes, quadriceps, calves, and supporting muscles will improve running economy and reduce injury risk over time.

### Things to improve

- Continue scheduling at least one active recovery day each week.
- Gradually increase strength training volume as your body adapts.
- Prioritize good technique over adding more repetitions.
