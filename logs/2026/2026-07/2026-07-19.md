# 🏃 Running Log — July 19, 2026

## 📋 Workout Summary

Today's goal was to complete my very first **10 KM Easy Run**.

The run started slower than usual as my body needed time to warm up, but after the first few kilometers I found a comfortable rhythm and maintained a steady pace for most of the session.

Around kilometer nine, I started running low on water and had to manage my hydration carefully. Despite that, I stayed mentally strong and crossed the finish line without stopping.

Completing my first double-digit distance is one of the biggest milestones in my running journey so far.

## 😊 Reflection

This run felt incredibly rewarding.

The beginning was challenging as my legs felt heavy during the first few kilometers, but I trusted the process and allowed my body to warm up naturally instead of forcing the pace.

Once everything settled, the run became surprisingly comfortable. I was even able to maintain a stable pace for several consecutive kilometers.

The biggest lesson today was hydration. Around kilometer nine, I realized that my 300 ml water bottle wasn't enough for a run of this distance. For future 10K and longer runs, carrying more water will become essential.

Crossing the 10K mark reminded me how much progress I've made. Just a few weeks ago, completing 5K was my biggest goal. Today, finishing 10 kilometers feels like the beginning of an even bigger journey.
